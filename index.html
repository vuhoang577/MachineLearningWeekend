Machine Learning Weekend

Instructor: Dr. David Sondak (Dassault Systèmes Simulia Corp.)

Organizers: Dr. Vu Hoang, Dr. Jose Morales (UTSA Department of Mathematics)

Student team leaders: Gaby Boada, Isaul Garcia, Sean Roberson

Are you a UTSA graduate student interested in machine learning? Join our intensive workshop where you'll dive headfirst into the exciting world of mathematical artificial intelligence.

Led by Dr. David Sondak, Research and Development Software Engineer in the Integrated Multiphysics group at Dassault Systèmes Simulia Corp., this hands-on experience will introduce you to machine learning algorithms.

Includes interactive sessions, practical exercises, and collaborative projects, gain invaluable insights and confidence to harness the potential of machine learning in your career or academic pursuits.

As a special bonus, Dr. Sondak will share his experience transitioning from academia to industry.

Register under: Registration

UTSA Graduate students from all departments are welcome. No prior knowledge of machine learning required. A bit of Python is helpful.  

Hosted by the Department of Mathematics at UTSA. We gratefully acknowledge the support by the UTSA Graduate School. Food and drink provided.
